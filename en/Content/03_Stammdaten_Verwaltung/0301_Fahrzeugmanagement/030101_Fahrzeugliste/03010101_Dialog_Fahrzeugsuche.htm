      Vehicle search dialog

      Determine the current location of a vehicle — at the depot with highlighting in the depot view , or on route via the vehicle dossier .

      Overview

      The Vehicle search returns the current location of a vehicle. Matches are highlighted visually in the depot view or shown in the vehicle dossier if the vehicle is on route.

      Open the dialog

      • In the depot view , click a free area → context menu → Vehicle search
      • Menu Edit → Vehicle search
      • Toolbar: Vehicle search button
      • Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+F

      Search criteria

      Criterion Description
      Vehicle number Direct search by internal vehicle ID/number.
      Train Search for coupled units (e.g., double train/consist).
      Block Select blocks of the current pull-out phase to which vehicles are assigned.

      Result display

      • Vehicle at depot: Opens the “Depot View” and briefly highlights the vehicle (blink).
      • Vehicle on route: Opens the vehicle dossier with the last reported location.

      Typical steps

      1. Open the Vehicle search dialog.
      2. Select a search criterion and enter/choose a value.
      3. Start the search — the result appears in the depot view or vehicle dossier.

      Tips

      • When searching by Block , choose the correct pull-out phase in the depot view.
      • Train search finds only complete units (no mix of single/double units).
      • No match? Check spelling/filters and the registration in ITCS/telematics.

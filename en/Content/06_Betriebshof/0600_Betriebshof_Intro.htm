      Depot – Overview

      This chapter bundles all functions related to the in PEAK.DMS: graphical location overview, maintenance of parking positions and , repositioning moves, driving orders, optional weather functions and charging management for e-buses (charging stations, charging orders, SOC monitoring). You will learn how to quickly grasp the current status and systematically control parking position and vehicle movements – including charging planning.

      What You Do in This Chapter

      • Read the and navigate to detail editing.
      • Maintain parking positions (block status, usage types, permitted vehicle types).
      • Manage (capacities, zones, infrastructure).
      • Create repositioning moves and prioritize driving orders.
      • Perform charging management: manage charging stations, plan charging orders, monitor SOC/remaining ranges.
      • Optionally view the weather forecast and record weather data.

      Target Group

      • – operational , order control, charging window.
      • Coordination/Service – / areas, charging points at the depot.
      • Administrators – Master data, display, and charging management parameters.

      Prerequisites

      • User permissions for , parking position editing, and (if used) charging management.
      • Created parking positions/areas/zones in the master data.
      • For e-buses: configured charging points/charging stations and maintained electrical master data (capacity, charging power, SOC parameters).

      E-Mobility – Special Notes

      • Operational usage types (e.g., Supply) mark charging/refueling areas in the .
      • Parking positions can be assigned to charging points/connectors; clearly mark charging areas.
      • Charging planning integrates timetable, SOC forecasts, and charging windows; weather conditions (frost/heat) influence energy demand and time buffer.

      Terms

      • Depot View: Graphical 2D overview of the with parking positions and vehicle status.
      • Usage Type: Operational role of a parking position (e.g., , , , Reserve, Storage Space).
      • Repositioning Move: Planned movement of a vehicle between parking positions/areas in the depot.
      • SOC (State of Charge): Charge status of the traction battery; basis for range and charging planning.
      • Charging Window: Time span during which a vehicle can be charged without violating specifications.

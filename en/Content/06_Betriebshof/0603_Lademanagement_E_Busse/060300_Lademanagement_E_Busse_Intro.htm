      Charge Management for E-Buses

      This chapter describes charge management in PEAK.DMS. You will learn to manage the charging infrastructure, plan charging orders, monitor charging statuses, and configure electrical parameters.

      SSB Concept: Charging & Depot Logic

      The charging specifications are integrated into depot logic and dispatching. Parking position and charging point allocation follow SSB rules on charging windows, priorities, and technical compatibility.

      Planning & Control

      • Planning according to pull-out/pull-in times and subsequent block
      • Automatic suggestions for bottlenecks (alternative charging point)
      • Monitoring of charging progress and deviations

      Role Reference

      Garage/Supply monitors charging progress and confirms deviations; Dispatching uses updated energy statuses for block allocation.

      What you will do in this chapter

      • Understand the basics of charge management (see Introduction to Charge Management).
      • Create, configure, and assign charging stations to parking positions (see Manage Charging Stations).
      • Plan and prioritize charging orders (see Plan Charging Orders).
      • Monitor charging statuses and ongoing charging processes (see Monitor Charging Statuses).
      • Maintain parameters and master data for E-Buses (see Parameterize electric vehicles).

      Target Audience

      • Dispatching/Control Center – plans charging processes and considers operational procedures.
      • Garage and Service – manages charging points, maintenance, and infrastructure.
      • Administrators – maintain master data, performance parameters, and rules.

      Prerequisites

      • User permissions for charging infrastructure and order planning.
      • Defined parking positions and vehicle master data in the system.
      • For E-Buses: Information on vehicle battery, charging points, and performance classes.

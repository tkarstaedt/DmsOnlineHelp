      “Depot Information” Dialog

      In the “” , you maintain central master data of a in PEAK.DMS – e.g., name, capacities, zones/tracks, infrastructure (car wash, , refueling station/charging points), and operational . Changes affect the display and in the as well as dependent .

      Opening the Dialog

      1. From the : Right-click in a free area → Edit …
      2. Alternatively: Via an administration or the of other objects (project-specific).

      Structure and Tabs

      The dialog is divided into tabs. Additional tabs may be present depending on the project configuration.

      • General – Basic data, visibility, and georeference.
      • Capacities – Number of parking positions and distribution across areas/tracks.
      • Layout & Zones – Areas, tracks, storage space, safety zones.
      • Infrastructure – Car wash, positions, , charging points.
      • Rules & Parameters – Standard usages, block times, .
      • Contact & Operational Data – Contact person, operating hours.

      “Capacities” Tab

      Define the usable parking positions and distribution key figures.

      Field Description
      Total Parking Positions Sum of all occupyable parking positions.

      Typical Actions

      Frequent maintenance tasks in the “” .

      1. Adjust Capacities: Open Capacities tab → update values → OK.
      2. Create New Zone: Layout & Zones tab → add area/track → assign designation.
      3. Assign Charging Points: Infrastructure tab → maintain Charging Points → assign zone/track.
      4. Define Standard Usages: Rules & Parameters tab → define usage types and block times.

      Save and Close

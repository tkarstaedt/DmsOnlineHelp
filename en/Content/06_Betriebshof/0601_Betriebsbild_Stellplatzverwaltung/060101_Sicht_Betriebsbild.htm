      SSB: Color Identifiers & Status

      The color coding of vehicle and area status is parameterized project-specifically for SSB. Meanings are based on internal SSB specifications (e.g., deployment, supply, block, deviation).

      • Deployment/Service-ready: highlighted in color as ready for service
      • Supply/Garage: separate color codes for charging/refueling and maintenance
      • Block/Quarantine: clearly distinct warning indicator

      SSB: Area and Track Designations

      Stabling areas, tracks, and sub-zones use internal SSB designations and number ranges (e.g., defined preferred areas). These affect filters, layers, and suggestion logic.

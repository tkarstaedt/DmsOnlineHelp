      Create Statistics

      In PEAK.DMS, you can create various dispatching statistics to evaluate processes and results. This chapter describes available statistic types, the creation procedure, and export and check options.

      Statistic Types

      • Block-related statistics: Pull-out and pull-in behavior, duty assignment.
      • Vehicle-related statistics: Utilization, availability, incidents.
      • Workshop statistics: Orders, downtimes, deadline overruns.
      • Supply statistics: Refueling, charging processes, cleaning.
      • Calendar-based statistics: Analyses by day types/operating days.

      Prerequisites

      • You are signed in to the DMS client.
      • You have permission to create statistics.
      • The relevant data (dispatching, workshop, supply) is recorded in the system.

      Procedure

      1. Open the Statistics module in DMS.
      2. Select the desired statistic type.
      3. Define the time period, area (e.g., depot, vehicle group), and filter.
      4. Click Create. The statistic is calculated and displayed.
      5. Check the results for completeness and plausibility.

      Filters & Options

      • Filter by date/operating day (incl. day types).
      • Filter by area (block, workshop, supply).
      • Additional options: Aggregation (day/week/month), level of detail, show/hide specific fields.

      Export Options

      • Excel (.xlsx): Further processing and pivot analyses.
      • CSV: Import into external systems.
      • PDF: Documentation and archiving.

      Quality Checks

      • Comparison with raw data in the source modules (dispatching, workshop, supply).
      • Random plausibility checks.
      • Verification of filter and aggregation settings.

      Notes & Best Practice

      • Create regular standard statistics (e.g., weekly vehicle availability).
      • Document filters deliberately before major analyses.
      • Add a version date to export files to facilitate later comparisons.

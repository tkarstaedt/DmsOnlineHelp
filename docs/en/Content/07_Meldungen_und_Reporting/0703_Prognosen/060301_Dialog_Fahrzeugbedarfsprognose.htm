      Dialog "Vehicle demand forecast"

      With the Vehicle demand forecast dialog you can determine the forecast fleet utilization of a depot for a specific calendar day. The basis is the vehicle type patterns from the imported block schedules.

      Objective

      Display of the expected number of vehicles by vehicle type, depot, and interval over the course of the day. The forecast is used for planning resources and vehicle availability.

      Prerequisites

      • Imported block data from DIVA (VDV 452) is available.
      • The period for which forecasts are available has been configured by the administrator.

      Open dialog

      1. In the Dialog toolbar, click the Vehicle demand forecast button.
      2. Or choose Edit > Vehicle demand forecast from the menu.

      Calculate forecast

      1. Select the calendar day in the calendar or spinner. Only days with available block data can be selected.
      2. Select the depot or mode.
      3. Select the desired interval (e.g., 15 min, 30 min, 60 min).
      4. Click Calculate to display the forecast.

      Presentation

      The results are displayed on two tabs:

      • Daily curve — time course of vehicle demand per interval; shows how many vehicles of a type are in block, pulling out, or pulling in.
      • Maximum vehicle demand — maximum number of vehicles required on the day considered.

      Notes on calculation

      • The forecast is based on block data for the next X days; the time grid is configurable.
      • Vehicles whose block starts, is active, or ends in the period considered are counted.
      • Multiple‑unit operations are counted as two vehicles.

      Example use

      To prepare the weekend timetable, a forecast for Sunday is created on Friday to determine the demand for articulated buses.

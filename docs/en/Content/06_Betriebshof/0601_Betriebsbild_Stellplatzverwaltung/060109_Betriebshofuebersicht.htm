      Depot Overview – Concept

      The in PEAK.DMS centrally summarizes essential information for , Service, and . It serves as an entry point for situation assessment and as a navigation hub to detail and .

      Depot Overlays & Summary (SSB)

      The overview can display SSB overlays (zones, bottlenecks, charging axes). Aggregated notes indicate priority conflicts, blocks, or missing compatibility.

      • Heatmap-like capacity/occupancy by area
      • Quick access to parking position and duty lineup information

      Purpose and Benefit

      • Quick overview of planning status, running orders, and vehicle statuses.
      • Early detection of critical situations (e.g., capacity bottlenecks, faults, delays).
      • Direct navigation to detailed topics (parking positions, , ).

      Typical Use Cases

      The overview supports different roles with compact information and targeted actions.

      • Dispatching: Check planned deployments, identify bottlenecks, trigger repositioning moves.
      • Service/Garage: Overview of occupancy and pending work.
      • Administration: Validate master data coherence, areas/zones, and capacities.

      Components of the Depot Overview

      The specific layout is project-specific. Common components include:

      • Planning Overview: Day/shift status (, ), Planned/Actual, .
      • Planned Deployments: Aggregation of today's relevant orders by priority/status.
      • Vehicle Overview: Availability, faults, reserves, E-bus notes (SOC/Window).
      • Garage Overview: Occupied bays, due work, release times.
      • Note/Message Area: Aggregated events with drill-down to the affected objects.
      • Maps/Widgets (optional): Location map, weather notes, charging/ windows.

      Filters and Time Control

      Global filters and time windows can be set for focus.

      • Time Period: now, next 2 hours, shift, today.
      • Area/Zone: Select subsets of the yard.
      • Order Type/Status: e.g., only repositioning moves "in progress".

      Interaction and Navigation

      All tiles and tables support drill-down into the responsible or .

      • Tile Click: opens the corresponding /detail table filtered by context.
      • Table Double-Click: opens the corresponding editing .
      • Context Menu: quick maintenance actions (block, permissions, trigger orders).

      Key Figures (Examples)

      The selection of is project-dependent; typical key figures are:

      • Pull-out Rate [%]: completed vs. planned at the current time.
      • Available Vehicles: service-ready vs. blocked/ -bound.
      • Open Orders: Number by type/status, proportion of overdue tasks.

      Roles & Permissions

      Displayed data and actions are based on user permissions.

      • Read Permissions: aggregated information.
      • Write Permissions: Maintenance actions and order creation.
      • Admin: Configuration of tiles, filters, and standard layouts.

      Configuration (Overview)

      Content and behavior can be adjusted administratively.

      • Tile Set: Selection of visible modules and their order.
      • Standard Filters: pre-set areas, time windows, order types.
      • Update: Interval for automatic refreshes (global or per tile).

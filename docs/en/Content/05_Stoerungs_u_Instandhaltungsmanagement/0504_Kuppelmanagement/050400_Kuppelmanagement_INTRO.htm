      Coupling Management

      Coupling management handles coupling and decoupling of vehicle pairs (trains) in daily operations. It includes signaling the train state (active/resolved), planned coupling orders with confirmation/cancellation, and provides clear operation paths, system effects, and auditability.

      Goals

      • Reliably operate blocks with suitable trains.
      • Unify operation: one dialog, two modes (couple/decouple) — for signaling and orders.
      • Make effects transparent: immediate effect on confirmation; flags on cancellation (orders).
      • Ensure evaluability (KPIs, exports).

      Context & dependencies

      • Dispatching Views — overview, filters, drill-down.
      • Manual Dispatching — conflict resolution & documentation.
      • Automatic Vehicle Dispatching — respects flags Do not couple/Do not decouple (from order cancellation) and considers the signaled train state.
      • Calendar: Blocks — timeline of events.

      Notes & best practices

      • Before confirming, check compatibility, locks, supply slots, and buffers.
      • On order cancellation, non-blocking markers Do not couple/Do not decouple are set; these influence automation proposals.
      • Signaling (couple/decouple) updates the operational train state immediately — review any divergent orders afterwards.
      • Document comments and reasons consistently to support evidence and statistics.

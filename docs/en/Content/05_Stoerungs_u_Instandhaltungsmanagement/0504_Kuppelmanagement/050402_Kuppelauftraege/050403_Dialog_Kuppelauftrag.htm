      Dialog: Coupling Order

      The dialog is used for Coupling and Decoupling equally. The title and order type adapt to the context (Couple vehicle, Decouple traction), while the fields and actions remain identical.

      One Dialog â€“ Two Modes

      In Couple vehicle mode, the planned coupling of a vehicle pair is confirmed or canceled. In Decouple traction mode, the dialog behaves identically but refers to the separation of the traction.

      Fields and Elements

      • Order Type: Shows the current mode (Couple or Decouple).
      • Traction: Identifier of the vehicle pair (e.g., 4185-4139).
      • Vehicles: Table with Number and Location of both vehicles.
      • Blocks: Affected /duties (if recognized), otherwise empty.
      • Actions: and ; additionally Close and Help.

      Procedure

      1. Check Rules & Compatibility.
      2. Check the traction, vehicles, and affected , if applicable.
      3. Click to signal to the DMS that the action has been executed, or to reject the action.

      System Effect

      • Confirm: The coupling or decoupling status is immediately adopted in the system. The vehicle pair appears accordingly as coupled or as decoupled in the , s, and calendars.
      • Cancel: Non-service-preventing fault markers are created for both vehicles. Depending on the type, they contain the flag Do not couple or Do not decouple. The Automatic Vehicle Dispatching respects these flags bindingly.

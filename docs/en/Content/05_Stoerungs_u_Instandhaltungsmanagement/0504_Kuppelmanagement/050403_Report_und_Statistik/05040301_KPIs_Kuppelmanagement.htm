      KPIs Coupling Management

      Evaluation of the most important key figures for coupling and decoupling procedures in PEAK.DMS – including definitions, formulas, filters, and references to export/documentation.

      Goals of the Evaluation

      • Create transparency regarding the quantity, quality, and timing of coupling/decoupling procedures.
      • Identify conflicts and bottlenecks (compatibility, blocks, time windows).
      • Measure the effect of process changes (e.g., automatic rules, depot logistics).

      Data Basis

      • : Status changes (Planned → Confirmed/Canceled), timestamp, source, comment.
      • Signaling: Reported traction status (exists/resolved) including time, source.
      • Conflict/Check Events: Compatibility, blocks, supply slots.

      Standard KPIs (Definitions & Formulas)

      KPIDefinitionFormulaNotes
      Confirmation RateProportion of confirmed orders out of all completed orders.Confirmed ÷ (Confirmed + Canceled)Optionally split by type (Couple/Decouple) and source (manual/automatic).
      Cancellation RateProportion of canceled orders out of all completed orders.Canceled ÷ (Confirmed + Canceled)High values indicate rule conflicts or incorrect specifications.
      Order Processing TimeTime from order creation to confirmation/cancellation.t(Confirmed|Canceled) − t(Created)Use median instead of mean, separate outliers.
      Conflict RateProportion of orders with at least one conflict (compatibility/block/slot).No. of orders with conflict ÷ No. of all ordersReport conflict classes separately.
      Time to Effectiveness (Signaling)Time between signaling and operational display in /calendars.t(Display) − t(Signaling)Should be close to 0; check deviations (interfaces/refresh).
      Flag Usage After CancellationFrequency of set flags Do not couple/Do not decouple after cancellation.No. of set flags ÷ No. of canceled orders Shows whether cancellation reasons are correctly secured.
      Re-opening RateProportion of orders that are newly created after cancellation.No. of new creations after cancellation ÷ No. of canceled ordersIndicates unclear rules or changing operational situation.

      Filters & Dimensions

      • Time Period (Day/Week/Month, Shift Window)
      • Type (Couple | Decouple)
      • Source (manual | automatic | Import)
      • Depot/Yard, Track, Line/Block
      • Vehicle Class, Traction Type

      Best Practices

      • Always display KPIs with Definition (tooltip or legend) to avoid misunderstandings.
      • Use Median and Percentiles for time-related KPIs; display outliers separately.
      • Report conflict classes separately (Compatibility, Block, Slot) to derive measures.
      • Reconcile Signaling vs. Order: In case of deviation, the reported signaling dominates; check contradictory orders.

      Export & Documentation

      Use the functions from Export Statistics to secure KPI data (CSV/Excel) for audits or further analyses.

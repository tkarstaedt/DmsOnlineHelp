      Supply track – terminals & displays

      Terminals and displays support drivers and depot staff during pull-in/out, servicing, and wayfinding on the depot.

      Pull-in and pull-out displays

      Displays guide vehicles when pulling into the supply track and release them after completion.

      • Pull-in: Show the next station (e.g., refuel, charge) and directions.
      • Intermediate status: Visualize "Waiting"/"In progress" per supply step.
      • Pull-out: Release message when the servicing sequence is complete.

      Driver terminals

      Driver terminals enable confirmations and provide wait-time information.

      • Accept/start a step by confirming at the terminal.
      • Safety and work instructions (e.g., engine off, enable charging).
      • Abort with reason (leads to re-queuing according to rules).

      Depot displays

      Overview displays support dispatch and workshop coordination.

      • Queue per station (current step, remaining duration, next vehicle).
      • Alarms for incidents, locks, or target violations (e.g., delayed pull-out).

      Message types & examples

      Type Example content
      Wayfinding "Please proceed to station Charge‑3".
      Safety/work instruction "Engine off, parking brake on, enable charging".
      Status "Waiting (approx. 6 min)".
      Release "Servicing complete — proceed to exit".

      Configuration & permissions

      Display logic and interaction are configurable.

      • Stations and display locations (pull-in, stations, pull-out).
      • Message priorities (safety > operations > comfort).
      • User permissions for start/stop, skip, re-queue.
